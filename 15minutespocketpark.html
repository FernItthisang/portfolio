Client: Magnolia Quality Development Corporation Limited (MQDC)

Part of: South Sukhumvit Project – Future Plans

My role: UX/UI Designer & Interactive Design, Boonmee Lab team

A map of all pocket parks in the South Sukhumvit area.

Overview

Developed for Magnolia Quality Development Corporation Limited (MQDC), 15 Minutes Pocket Park is the final phase of the South Sukhumvit project and part of the Future Plans section. The website showcases the location and information of upcoming pocket parks in the South Sukhumvit area, including nearby transportation and recommended activities. While it uses the same color scheme as the main website, its colorful illustrations for different parks create a more vibrant and playful mood.

Technology & Process

To support the addition of more parks in the future, our development team selected Airtable as the content management system (CMS). However, direct import of map locations from Google Maps was not possible, and the CMS proved challenging for novice users. To address this, our team built a custom conversion tool and streamlined data workflows, making it easier for MQDC to manage park data and ensure smooth updates.

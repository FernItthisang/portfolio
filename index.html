Turtle Guardian

Case Study UX/UI DesignData VizInteractive

This website raises awareness about sea turtle conservation in Thailand through interactive storytelling. It features Turtle Guardian, a quiz that teaches protection strategies; Turtle Watch, a dashboard of nesting data (2020–2022); and Human Wave, an analysis of 4,000+ Thai social media posts.
